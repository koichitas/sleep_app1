## Full Description (4000 chars max)

```
The game that puts you to sleep — on purpose.

Can't fall asleep? Still scrolling at midnight? Doze Panels turns bedtime into a gentle brain workout that naturally leads you to drift off. No meditation, no white noise — just numbers.

Tap numbers in order. Brain gets tired. Sleep happens.


■ How It Works

Numbers are scattered across the screen. Your job: tap them in order, starting from 1.

Simple — but with a twist. Correct numbers don't disappear. Every tapped number stays on screen, so you must remember where you left off while scanning for the next one. That mental effort — searching, remembering, searching again — gradually wears your brain down.

Correct tap: flashes green. Wrong tap: flashes red. Keep going until your eyelids feel heavy.


■ Stages That Grow With You

Start easy. Get sleepier as you go.

▶ Stage 1 (4×4 — 16 numbers)
Tapped numbers stay green so you always know your progress. Perfect for warming up — or for nights when you're already halfway asleep.

▶ Stage 2 (5×5) onward
The green fades after one second. Now you have to remember on your own. As stages progress, the number count grows and each panel gets smaller — your eyes work harder, your brain works harder. Difficulty increases just enough to keep you engaged without ever frustrating you.

▶ Final Stage (10×10 — 100 numbers)
One hundred numbers. Tiny panels. Eyes squinting, brain straining. If you reach this point without dozing off, give it another try — sleep has a way of catching up.


■ The In-Between Moments Are Part of the Design

After clearing each stage, there's a short pause before the next one begins.

Your brain just worked hard. Now it gets to rest. That quiet moment — screen softly glowing, nothing to do — is often exactly when sleep sneaks in.


■ Stuck? Use a Hint

Miss the same number three times in a row and a dialog appears. You can keep trying on your own, or watch a short ad to reveal the next correct number in green.

Either way, feel free to fall asleep right after.


■ Sleep Detection

No activity for 10 minutes? The app decides you've fallen asleep — and congratulates you.

A message appears and you're returned to the start screen. Waking up to find you passed out mid-game? That's the perfect ending.


■ Track Your Patterns

Your recent sessions are shown on the start screen:

· Doze off — how many minutes before you fell asleep
· Clear — total time to beat all stages
· Quit — closed the app before finishing

Over time, you might start to notice when and how quickly sleep finds you.


■ Time Attack Mode

Couldn't sleep tonight? Challenge yourself to beat your fastest clear time instead. No servers, no leaderboards — just you against yourself.

When your time improves, you'll know your brain is getting sharper. Which makes it even more satisfying the next time you doze off mid-run.


■ Who Is This For?

· People who struggle to fall asleep or have insomnia
· Anyone who wants a consistent, calming bedtime routine
· Those who naturally fall asleep with a screen in hand
· Fans of light brain training or number puzzles
· Anyone who just wants to stop doomscrolling at night

If you can count, you can play. All ages welcome.

Tonight, put down the endless scroll. Pick up the numbers. Wake up tomorrow.
```
